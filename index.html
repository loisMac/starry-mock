Our story

Founded in 2021, built on precision and clarity.

Northline Studio started with a simple idea: create a space where careful barbering meets design-forward thinking. No unnecessary ceremony. No pretence. Just honest craftsmanship and a space that doesn't look like every other barber shop. We've focused on training specialists and investing in the small details—the kind of details that mean your haircut still looks good three weeks later.
